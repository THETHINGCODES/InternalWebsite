Skills that you need are:

You will need quick reaction speeds, good hand to eye coordination, and fast responses.

WASD

The WASD keys are the most commanly used keys in gaming; W = forward, A = left, S = backward, and D = right. These are the directions that you will move in if you press these buttons.

Mouse

On every mouse, there are at least two buttons. Two main ones at least; the right click button and left click button. The right click button generally uses the object e.g. shoots; while the left click button will usually aim.
